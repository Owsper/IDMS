Overview

IDMS supports organization workflows for members and administrators. Use it to manage profiles, documents, voting, meetings, notifications, finances, activity reports, imports, and bug tracking.

Getting Started

  1. Register for an account.
  2. Verify your email when prompted.
  3. Sign in and complete your profile.
  4. Use Dashboard to open the workflows available to your role.

Member Tasks

  • Update profile details and notification preference.
  • Browse and download approved documents.
  • Vote in eligible active voting events.
  • Review meetings, agendas, and minutes.
  • Report defects from Bug Tracking.

Admin Tasks

  • Manage members, imports, documents, and categories.
  • Create voting events and export results.
  • Schedule meetings, record attendance, and upload minutes.
  • Record transactions, monitor budgets, and export finance reports.
  • Review activity summaries and verify bug closure.

Finance

Finance supports income and expense tracking, generated reports, category budgets, threshold alerts, recent transactions, and CSV exports.

Activity Summary

Activity Summary provides daily, weekly, and monthly widgets, highlights, module breakdowns, timeline bars, and recent activity feeds.

Bug Tracking

Bug reports include severity, priority, module, environment, build/version, reproducibility, assignment, steps, expected behavior, actual behavior, fix notes, and verification notes.
