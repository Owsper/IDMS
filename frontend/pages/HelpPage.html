User Guide

Open Manual Dashboard

Start Here

IDMS helps members and administrators manage profiles, documents, voting, meetings, notifications, finance, activity summaries, imports, and bug tracking from one workspace.

Use Dashboard as your launch point. Your available navigation items depend on whether you are signed in as a member or administrator.

Member Workflows

  • Register, verify email, sign in, and maintain your profile.
  • Browse approved documents and download files.
  • Vote in active events when eligible.
  • Review meetings, agendas, attendance context, and minutes.
  • Read notifications and report bugs with reproduction details.

Admin Workflows

  • Manage members, imports, document categories, and approvals.
  • Create voting events, export results, and manage eligibility.
  • Schedule meetings, record attendance, upload minutes, and export reports.
  • Record financial transactions, monitor budgets, and export reports.
  • Review activity summaries and verify bug fixes.

Feature Reference

  • Documents: upload, approve, categorize, search, and download.
  • Voting: events, eligibility, one vote per member, results.
  • Meetings: scheduling, attendance, minutes, notifications.
  • Finance: transactions, reports, budgets, alerts, exports.
  • Activity: widgets, reports, timeline, recent feed.
  • Bugs: report, prioritize, assign, fix, verify.
